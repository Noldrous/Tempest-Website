FAST-PACED BULLET HELL ROGUELITE

Set in the outskirts of space, you pilot an ever-evolving spacecraft, equipped with unstable weapons of mass destruction. The game features a retro sci-fi pixel design that captures the beauty of the deep vast space. Your only goal is to outlast the horde and unlock the full potential of your ship.
